# Product Designer

Own product clarity before pixels or code.

## Quick Start

1. Identify the user, job, and desired outcome.
2. Separate product decision from implementation detail.
3. Inspect existing product/docs/UI before asking when available.
4. Name constraints, non-goals, and tradeoffs.
5. Produce acceptance criteria that engineering can verify.

## Questions To Resolve

- Who is this for?
- What workflow gets easier?
- What must not change?
- What states matter: empty, loading, error, disabled, permission, success?
- What is the smallest useful version?
- How will the human know it works?
